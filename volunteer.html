Join as Volunteer

Help shape Sikkim's future by becoming part of our community

Why Volunteer?

Help shape Sikkim's future! Volunteers assist with surveys, documentation, and advocacy. No special qualifications needed - just a desire to help and basic smartphone skills. Contact us to join.

What You'll Do

  • Field Surveys: Visit locations where amenities are requested to verify needs
  • Documentation: Take photos, gather community signatures, and document requirements
  • Community Outreach: Inform citizens about the platform and how it works
  • Review & Verification: Help review submitted requests for completeness
  • Advocacy: Support community proposals to authorities

Requirements

  • Resident of Sikkim
  • Basic smartphone skills (camera, WhatsApp/Telegram)
  • Commitment of a few hours per week (flexible schedule)
  • Passion for community development
  • No prior experience needed - we'll train you!

How to Join

Interested in volunteering? Send us an email with your name, district, and a brief note about why you'd like to volunteer. We'll get back to you within 48 hours with next steps.

Email: whatsikkimesewant@tutamail.com
Subject: Volunteer Application - [Your District]
