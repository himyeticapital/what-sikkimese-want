How It Works

1

Submit Your Request

Fill out the form with your location, amenity type, and why it's needed. More details help us assess better.

2

Community Review

Volunteers review within 48-72 hours, verify details, check for duplicates, and categorize by priority.

3

Ground Survey

Local volunteers conduct on-site surveys, gather photos, community signatures, and document infrastructure gaps.

4

Advocacy & Follow-up

We submit formal proposals to authorities and keep you updated via your reference ID.

FAQ

How does it work?

Submit your request, get a Reference ID, and track status. We compile approved requests and submit to authorities.

Is it free?

Yes, completely free. We're volunteers working to improve Sikkim's infrastructure.

Can I submit multiple requests?

Yes! Submit as many as needed. Each gets a unique reference ID for tracking.
